What is Chate?

As a group of students from Myanmar attending top global universities, we created "ချိတ်" as a passion project to share our knowledge and experience to other students - all for free. Our team includes students from institutions such as MIT, Brown, Darthmouth, Tufts, and Swarthmore

Our Mission

The “ချိတ်” project aims to connect Myanmar students with the world of international education through peer-to-peer guidance on admissions, scholarships, and financial aid.

Our Vision

By offering free advising, webinars, blogs, and resources, our goal is to create opportunities by helping other Myanmar students navigate the college admissions process.
